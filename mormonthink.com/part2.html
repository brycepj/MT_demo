Unique Textual Parallels

from the Oberlin Spalding Ms. 
and the Book of Mormon


Part 2: (Textual Parallels)

Listing by Dale R. Broadhurst
From Data Compiled by
Bill A. Williams, jr.


The following 37 words are found in the Book of Mormon and the Oberlin Spalding Ms., but do not appear in the KJV Old Testament, Apocrypha, or New Testament. Neither can they be found in the writings of Josephus nor in Ethan Smith's View of the Hebrews. For this reason they are considered as being "relatively unique" to Spalding and the "Nephite Record." The genesis of this compilation is discussed in Part 1: (Introduction and List). The various occurrence patterns formed by these words in their respective texts are examined in Part 3: (Charts and Tabulation). Several examples of these particular parallels, viewed within the context of constellations of vocabulary and phraseology similarities in the two works, are available for examination in Part 4: (Textual Samples) of this presentation.
